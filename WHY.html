WHY DO PEOPLE HACK

In general, people may engage in hacking for a variety of reasons, both benign and malicious. Some possible motivations include:
1.Curiosity and the desire to learn about computer systems and security
2.Activism or political motivations, such as hacktivism
3.Financial gain, through activities like theft, fraud, or ransomware
4.Causing disruption or damage, out of malice or as a form of protest
5.Seeking attention or notoriety in the hacking community
